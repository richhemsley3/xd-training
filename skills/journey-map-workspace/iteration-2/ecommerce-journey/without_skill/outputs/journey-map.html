Customer Actions
  • Searches for products via search engine or browsing categories
  • Scrolls through product listings and filters results
  • Clicks on items of interest and views product images
  • Reads detailed product descriptions and specs
  • Checks customer reviews and star ratings
  • Compares prices and features across products
  • Adds favorites to wishlist or cart
  • Adds final items to cart and reviews order
  • Enters shipping address and payment info
  • Applies promo codes or discounts
  • Confirms and places the order
  • Receives order confirmation email
  • Tracks shipment status via app or link
  • Contacts support if delays occur
  • Receives and inspects the package
  • Uses and evaluates the product
  • Writes a review or rates the product
  • Contacts support for returns or issues
  • Responds to follow-up marketing emails
Customer Thinking
"I need a new pair of headphones. Let me see what's out there." "There are so many options... I'll narrow it down."

"Why do I need to create an account just to check out?" "These extra fees weren't mentioned before..."

"This is exactly what I wanted, I'll leave a review." "If returns are easy, I'd buy from here again."

Pain Points &
Opportunities
⚠ Overwhelming number of choices causes decision fatigue
✓ Personalized recommendations based on browsing history
⚠ Inconsistent product information across listings
✓ Side-by-side comparison tool with clear spec tables
⚠ Checkout friction: forced account creation, too many form fields
⚠ Hidden costs: unexpected shipping fees and taxes at final step
✓ Guest checkout, saved payment methods, transparent pricing
⚠ Delivery delays: missed estimated delivery windows
⚠ Lack of real-time tracking updates; status goes stale
✓ Proactive SMS/push notifications with live tracking
⚠ Complex returns process discourages repeat purchases
✓ Easy one-click returns and loyalty program enrollment
